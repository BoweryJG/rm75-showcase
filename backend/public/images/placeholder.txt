Place your watch images in this directory with the following naming convention:
- rm-075-01-front.jpg
- rm-075-01-back.jpg
- rm-075-01-side.jpg
- rm-075-02-front.jpg
- rm-075-02-back.jpg
- rm-075-02-side.jpg

Recommended image specifications:
- Format: JPEG or WebP
- Resolution: 1200x1200px minimum
- Optimization: Compress images for web use
- Background: Transparent or consistent color
